REFUND POLICY

Cancellation or refund policy is approved on a case-to-case basis at the discretion of the management.

If the student wishes to cancel any Offline/Online course after registration and payment of fees, an administration fee of Rs.2500 & GST 18% will be deducted, and the remaining amount will be refunded.
Course cancellation must be requested at least ten (10) working days before the course commencement date.

The same policy applies to students who have paid the entire course fee. If a student discontinues in the middle of the course, no refund will be provided.

Students are not eligible for any refund if the course cancellation request is made less than ten (10) working days before the course commencement date.

If KISS TECH cancels an event/course, a 100% refund will be paid to the student within ten (10) days from the announcement date of cancellation.

KISS TECH reserves the right to postpone/cancel an event, or change the event location due to insufficient enrollments, instructor illness, or force majeure events (like floods, cyclones, earthquakes, epidemic or pandemic outbreak, etc.) or any other unfortunate situation. This will be communicated in writing.

Note: The mode of refund may vary depending on circumstances.
- For refunds via Credit/Debit Card or Net Banking, it may take ten (10) to fourteen (14) working days to appear in the student’s account. This delay is due to bank policies beyond KISS TECH's control.
- For refunds via UPI or online transactions like IMPS, it will take 24 hours to reflect in the student’s account.

How to Request a Refund:
To request a refund, simply email us your details (Batch Code, Name, Phone No), including the reason for the refund request. We value customer feedback and use it to improve our service quality continuously.
